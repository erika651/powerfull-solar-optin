Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Powerfull Solar (“Powerfull Solar”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or submit your information through our forms.

Information We Collect

When you voluntarily submit information through our website, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Information related to your energy consultation or service inquiry

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries you initiate
  • Provide energy audits, solar consultations, and related services
  • Schedule appointments and provide requested follow-up communication
  • Communicate with you via email or SMS only after explicit consent is provided

SMS Communications & Consent

If you provide your mobile phone number and explicitly opt in via a required checkbox on our website forms, Powerfull Solar may send you SMS messages related to your inquiry, including:

  • Energy audits
  • Solar consultations
  • Scheduling and appointment reminders
  • Follow-up communication related to your request

Message frequency varies.
Message and data rates may apply.
You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.

We do not send unsolicited messages, cold outreach, or messages using purchased, rented, or shared lists. SMS messages are sent only in response to a user-initiated inquiry.

Opt-In & Record Keeping

Consent to receive SMS messages is collected before any messages are sent. Opt-in consent may be logged and stored to demonstrate compliance with carrier and regulatory requirements.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including mobile phone numbers, with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may only be shared:

  • To provide services you have requested
  • When required by law or legal process

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access to or correction of your personal information

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
